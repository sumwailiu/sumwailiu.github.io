AI-empowered Base station energy-saving platform

To help China achieve the “dual carbon” goal, our platform has provided data analyses and intelligent control services for millions of 4/5G base stations and hundreds of data centers over more than 240 cities. The scale is increasing.

2019.10-present:

  1. Energy-saving control on 4/5G base station via Reinforcement Learning
  • The parameters related to power-saving are optimized under RL algorithms;
  • The function has been deployed in 25 provinces across the country. With users’ experience satisfied, the energy-saving effect is boosted by nearly 50%. 285 million kWh has been saved;
  • One patent was submitted.
  1. Missing data imputation with Generative Adversarial Networks
  • WGAN was applied to learn the distribution of the data of the access network, to fill the missing parts of the collected data from base stations;
  • Compared with the expert-designed method, this achieved better results on downstream tasks.
  1. Large-scale hyperparameter optimization for time-series model
  • The hyperparameters of millions of predictive models are optimized by evolutionary algorithms;
  • After the hyperparameter search, the prediction error is reduced by 15~30%.
  1. Time series forecast for 4G base stations
  • Predictive models are trained to support the analysis and alarm functions of the platform;
  • In terms of scale, one model per base station, and millions of models are trained distributedly;
  • In terms of accuracy, we achieve comparable results to that of Huawei.
